US passport photos must be exactly 2×2 inches (51×51mm) with a white background. The head height must be between 1 inch and 1⅜ inches from chin to top of head. The photo must be taken within the last 6 months, with a neutral expression and both eyes open. WithSwag auto-crops to these exact specifications.
For a Schengen tourist visa you typically need: a completed application form, two biometric photos (35×45mm), valid passport, travel medical insurance (minimum €30,000 coverage), flight reservation, hotel booking, bank statements (last 3 months), employment letter, and a cover letter. Requirements vary slightly by country. Use our Document Checklist tool for a personalized list.
US passport processing takes 6–8 weeks for routine service, or 2–3 weeks with expedited processing ($60 extra). UK passports take up to 10 weeks. EU countries vary from 2–6 weeks depending on the country. India takes 1–3 weeks for normal and 1–3 days for Tatkal. Always apply well before your travel date.
Yes, travel medical insurance is mandatory for all Schengen visa applications. The policy must cover at least €30,000 in medical expenses, including emergency medical evacuation and repatriation. It must be valid for the entire duration of your stay plus a few extra days. Compare options with our Insurance Comparison tool.
